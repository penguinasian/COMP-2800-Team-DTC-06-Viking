About Us

According to the Vancouver's Greenest City Action Plan, one of the goals of the City is related to Green Transportation. The first target according to Greenest City 2020 Action plan is to "Make the majority of trips (over 50%) by foot, bicycle and public transit". Besides, by helping residents to use their bikes more frequently, either as a day-to-day commuting alternative for reaching their work place, shopping, and meeting their family and friends, or for sport, practice and fun, this will help the city to reach its Zero Carbon, Zero waste, and a healthier ecosystem overarching goals.
As Vikings developement team interested both in biking and the city's environment, we believe by providing this app to Vancouver residents, we can be helpful in enhancing your quality of biking, and at the same time help our city's environment and its resident's involment in enhancing their quality of life.
